BBM [Balance Board Math]

Learn math through balance and movement.

Getting Started

You will need to do three things:

  1. Grab a balance board
  2. Assemble an angle sensor kit
  3. Attach the angle sensor kit onto your balance board

To confirm you're good to go, look for a red flashing light on the microcontroller. For more information, visit Getting Started Guide.
